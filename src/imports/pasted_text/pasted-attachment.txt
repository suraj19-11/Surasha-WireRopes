Design a complete modern B2B manufacturing website for "Surasha Wireropes" — a wire 
rope manufacturer and wholesaler based in Thane, Maharashtra, India (est. 2020). 
Take strong design inspiration from fortran.in: editorial typographic layouts, full-bleed 
dark hero sections, clean white product pages, numbered section markers, generous 
whitespace, and a premium industrial feel. NOT a marketplace/IndiaMART-style UI.

BRAND & DESIGN SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Color Palette:
  Primary Dark:     #0D1B2A  (hero, navbar, footer — near black navy)
  Secondary Dark:   #1A2E45  (section backgrounds, alternate rows)
  Accent Orange:    #E87722  (all CTAs, highlights, hover states, price text)
  Accent Light:     #F5A623  (hover variant of orange)
  White:            #FFFFFF  (cards, forms, body)
  Off-White:        #F8F7F4  (alternate sections, like fortran's cream)
  Text Primary:     #1A1A1A
  Text Secondary:   #555555
  Border:           #E2E2E2
  Steel Gray:       #8A9BAD  (used for labels, secondary text on dark bg)

Typography:
  Headings:         "Inter" or "Poppins", weight 700, tracked slightly wide
  Subheadings:      weight 500
  Body:             "Inter", weight 400, line-height 1.7
  Accent Labels:    ALL CAPS, letter-spacing 0.15em, 12px, steel gray color
  Prices:           weight 700, orange, 24px+

Design Language (from fortran.in reference):
  - Large full-bleed hero with dark overlay and industrial background photo
  - Numbered section markers ("01", "02", "03") in orange before section titles
  - Clean white product cards with minimal borders, hover lift effect
  - Section intro: small ALL CAPS label + large bold headline + body paragraph
  - "Get Quote" sticky CTA button in navbar (orange, rounded pill)
  - Alternating white / off-white (#F8F7F4) section backgrounds
  - Footer: dark navy, multi-column, clean link lists
  - Breadcrumb navigation on all inner pages
  - Thin orange underline accent on active nav items
  - Product detail pages: 2-column, image gallery left + specs table right
